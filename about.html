🙋 About Avix Mobile

Your trusted phone & accessories store in Port Harcourt

Our Story

Avix Mobile Accessories started as a small shop in Port Harcourt with a simple mission: to provide quality phones and accessories at affordable prices. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted destination for Tecno, Itel, Redmi, and Samsung devices.

We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable technology. That's why we offer both new and refurbished phones — fully tested and backed by our warranty. Whether you need a phone, a charger, a screen guard, or a repair, we've got you covered.
